Can alternate-day fasting rewire our cognitive function via the gut microbiome?
Systems Analogy: Restructures the peripheral gut database to reprogram resident microglial security patrols.
Biological Mechanism: A 2025 RCT of 96 adults demonstrated that Alternate Day Fasting (ADF) improves cognitive performance and reduces neuroinflammation. This effect is microbiome-dependent, as fecal transplants (FMT) from fasting participants transferred these benefits to mice by activating microglial phagocytic capacity and suppressing inflammatory cascades.
