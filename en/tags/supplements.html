DHA Target Engagement & Cognition in Dementia Risk BIOLOGICAL CIRCUITS

Does raising brain DHA levels actually slow down cognitive decline? Takeaway: A 24-month RCT of 365 older adults demonstrated that while high-dose DHA (2g/day) successfully crosses the blood-brain barrier, it does not improve cognitive scores, memory, or brain volumes. Isolated supplements cannot override systemic metabolic aging and genetic risks.
