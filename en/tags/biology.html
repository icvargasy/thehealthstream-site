DHA Target Engagement & Cognition in Dementia Risk BIOLOGICAL CIRCUITS

Does raising brain DHA levels actually slow down cognitive decline? Takeaway: A 24-month RCT of 365 older adults demonstrated that while high-dose DHA (2g/day) successfully crosses the blood-brain barrier, it does not improve cognitive scores, memory, or brain volumes. Isolated supplements cannot override systemic metabolic aging and genetic risks.

Fasting, Microbiome Shifts, and Neuroinflammation BIOLOGICAL CIRCUITS

Can alternate-day fasting rewire our cognitive function via the gut microbiome? Takeaway: A 2025 RCT of 96 adults demonstrated that Alternate Day Fasting (ADF) improves cognitive performance and reduces neuroinflammation. This effect is microbiome-dependent, as fecal transplants (FMT) from fasting participants transferred these benefits to mice by activating microglial phagocytic capacity and suppressing inflammatory cascades.
