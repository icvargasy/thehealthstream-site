Outlive: Science & Art of Longevity

#book#longevity#exercise

1-Min Takeaway Pill

True longevity requires prioritizing healthspan alongside lifespan, focusing on cardiorespiratory fitness (VO2 max) and muscle mass as primary protective shields against the chronic diseases of aging.

Epistemic Consensus Scale
High Controversy Developing Hypothesis Established Consensus

Peter Attia's Outlive calls for a shift from reactive Medicine 2.0 to proactive Medicine 3.0. The core message is that modern medicine is excellent at postponing death but poor at extending healthspan. To combat the four horsemen of aging (cardiovascular disease, cancer, neurodegenerative disease, and metabolic syndrome), individuals must build high cardiorespiratory fitness through Zone 2 training and intensive strength routines. Preserving functional biology into the centenary decade requires intentional early training.
