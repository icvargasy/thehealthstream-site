colibactin

✓ Verified HumanType: molecule
Systems Analogy

A malicious software patch released by an invasive bot that actively cuts database instruction code, introducing permanent mutations.

A genotoxic chemical metabolite produced by Escherichia coli strains carrying the pks pathogenicity island, which induces double-strand DNA breaks and mutational signatures in host mucosal cells.

Scientific Sources & Verbatim Definitions

  • Pleguezuelos-Manzano C, et al. Mutational signature in colorectal cancer caused by genotoxic E. coli. Nature. 2020;580(7802):269-273. (Page 269)
    "Colibactin-producing E. coli strains induce double-stranded DNA breaks in mammalian cells."
