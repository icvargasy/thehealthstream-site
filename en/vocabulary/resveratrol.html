resveratrol

✓ Verified HumanType: molecule
Systems Analogy

A metabolic bypass switch that plugs into **SIRT1** controllers to accelerate DNA repair and efficiency routines.

A dietary polyphenol found in red grape skins that acts as an activator of the SIRT1 deacetylase, mimicking some physiological aspects of caloric restriction.
