antioxidant

Type: molecule
Aliases: antioxidants antioxidant defense
Systems Analogy

Anti-rust coating applied to metal components to prevent chemical corrosion.

A molecule capable of neutralizing reactive oxygen species (ROS) and free radicals, thereby protecting cellular structures from oxidative degradation.
