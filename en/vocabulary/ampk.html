AMPK

✓ Verified HumanType: protein
Systems Analogy

The cell's primary fuel gauge that monitors energy balance and switches the system from storage into cleanup mode when fuel runs low.

AMP-activated protein kinase; a conserved master energy sensor that regulates glucose and lipid metabolism, and triggers autophagy under low energy status.
