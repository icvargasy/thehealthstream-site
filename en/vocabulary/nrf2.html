Nrf2

Type: protein
Aliases: nuclear factor erythroid 2-related factor 2
Systems Analogy

The master coordinator of emergency response protocols that runs diagnostics and activates anti-corrosion defense shields.

Nuclear factor erythroid 2-related factor 2; a Nuclear factor that coordinates the gene expression of cytoprotective, antioxidant, and phase II detoxification enzymes.
