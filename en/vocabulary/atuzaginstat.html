atuzaginstat

✓ Verified HumanType: molecule
Systems Analogy

A targeted molecular patch designed to lock onto toxic protease outputs and neutralize them before they dissolve processor circuits.

COR388; an experimental, brain-penetrant small-molecule inhibitor of Porphyromonas gingivalis Lys-gingipains, developed to block neurodegeneration in Alzheimer's.

Scientific Sources & Verbatim Definitions

  • ClinicalTrials.gov study. GAIN Trial: Phase 2/3 study of atuzaginstat in mild-to-moderate Alzheimer's disease. NCT03828240. (Clinical Overview)
    "Atuzaginstat selectively inhibits gingipain-induced toxicity to protect neural structures."
