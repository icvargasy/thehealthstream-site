We hold a deep commitment to reader privacy and transparency. Because our website operates as a zero-runtime static site, we protect your data by design.

### 1. Minimal Data Collection
We do not track you, run database servers, or host user profile databases.
- **Static Access**: Browsing our articles and lexicon demands no registration, logins, or cookie tracking.
- **No Third-Party Analytics**: We do not use intrusive third-party behavioral trackers or target advertising services.

### 2. Form Operations & Third-Party Integrations
To support community feedback and voting without introducing complex server runtimes, we rely on secure, privacy-conscious routing:
- **Google Forms**: When you submit a protocol proposal or contact us, your submission is routed directly to a Google Form. We only collect the fields you choose to fill out.
- **Local Storage**: Backlog votes and theme settings are stored locally on your device using browser `localStorage`. No vote identification data is shared or transmitted to us other than the aggregated counts tallies.

### 3. Contact and Updates
If you have questions about our static infrastructure or privacy practices, please reach out via our [Contact Page](contact.html).
