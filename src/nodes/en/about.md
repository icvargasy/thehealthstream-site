We map physiological networks, metabolic circuits, and behavioral protocols. Our objective is to make complex biology accessible, understandable, and actionable at every level—from the molecular interactions of cellular pathways up to daily lifestyle habits.

### Core Tenet: Systems Biology
We view the human body not as a collection of isolated symptoms, but as an interconnected circuit board of biological feedback loops. Every decoding on this site maps these pathways upstream and downstream. By understanding the chemical signals that govern our cellular networks, we can make informed, deliberate choices about our daily habits, our longevity protocols, and our long-term health.

### Independent & Transparent
To protect our independence and serve our readers objectively, we follow these tenets:
- **Product Reviews & Sponsorships**: We will only recommend commercial products if we believe the value of the benefits is genuine. Any such recommendations, sponsorships, or partnerships will always be explicitly declared. We maintain independent opinions and cannot be swayed by corporate sponsors.
- **Evidence Grade**: An **Evidence Grade** framework rating the quality of research supporting an entry, ranging from High to Very Low.
- **Evidence & Studies**: An **Evidence & Studies** section summarizing supporting trials, cohort sizes, and key outcomes.
- **Jargon Popovers**: Interactive jargon popovers injecting precise molecular definitions directly into the text.
